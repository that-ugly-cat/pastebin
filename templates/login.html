How it works

Your password is your account. There are no usernames — the password you choose is the only key to your pastebin.

Choose a password to create a new pastebin. Use the same password later to open it again from any device.

Inside, you can store any number of titled text items — notes, snippets, links, anything. Items can be created, edited, and deleted.

Don't lose your password. There is no recovery mechanism.
